The Milano Ultrasonic Aromatherapy Diffuser & Humidifier creates an aromatherapy oasis in any space of the home or office!

Designed with the ultimate in ultrasonic technology this product quietly fills the room with a pleasantly cooling ultra-fine aromatic mist that freshens any space with the added benefit of also adding moisture to the air with its humidifier features.

The ultrasonic technology used in the diffuser works by breaking up the combined mixture of water and essential oil into millions of micro-particles which then diffuse into the surrounding air with the activated ingredients from the essential oil. Under this richly heightened effect of aromatherapy your mind will be calmed and relaxed and the air around you enhanced.

The product features an easy-to-fill 200ml tank that lasts for up to 4 hours of continuous duration or 7 hours in intermittent mode. It also has in-built colour-changing 12 LED mood lights that add to the ambience and serves as a great night-light for bedside use and for added comfort.
